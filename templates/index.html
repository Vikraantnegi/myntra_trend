What we Offer?

  • CGANN Generation
  • MNIST Generation
  • Trendy Score

Using CGANN, we are generating trending clothes by taking some attributes for the clothing like different categories of the clothing such as T-Shirts, Shirts, Dresses, Shoes, etc, if you want sleeve or not, how much sleeve length you want, how much skin exposure you want, etc. All of this data is taken from the user and then the model helps in generating images of clothing related to your choices, keeping the trends in mind.

Attribute Dataset!

In this feature, we are generating trending clothes by taking only category of the clothing from you such as T-Shirts, Shirts, Dresses, Shoes, etc, and then the model helps in generating images of clothing related to your choices, keeping the trends in mind.

MNIST Dataset!

In this feature, we ask you to upload an image of some clothing of yours, and by analysing what you are wearing, how much relevant the clothes are, and how much trendy are they in today's scenario. Keeping those trends in mind, we compare them to your outfit an then We generate a score out of 100 for your dressing and clothing.
